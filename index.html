About

Ottawa's Gourmet
Seafood Truck

As part of Ottawa's diverse gourmet street food movement, Ad Mare (latin, pronounced /ad ˈma.re/ or “ahd MAH-ray” ) has been serving sustainable prepared-on-the-spot fish and seafood dishes since 2013.

From crispy fish and chips, to loaded fish tacos, we bring the flavours of the coast to the heart of the city.

Fish & Chips

Reusable takeout

We use sustainable packaging

Ask for a reusable Friendlier container when you order. Return containers to any Friendlier location (including the box in front of the truck) so they can be washed, reused, and kept out of the single-use stream.
