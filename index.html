About

Ottawa's Gourmet
Seafood Truck

As part of Ottawa's diverse gourmet street food movement, Ad Mare (latin, pronounced /ad ˈma.re/ or “ahd MAH-reh”) has been serving prepared-on-the-spot fish and seafood dishes since 2013.

From crispy fish and chips, to loaded fish tacos, we bring the flavours of the coast to the heart of the city.

Example menu

Our menu changes frequently. Here's what you can typically expect.

Fish & Chips

Our classic. Fresh fish in a crispy golden batter, served with potato, sweet potato, and beet chips and truck-made tartar sauce.

Soft tortillas loaded with seasoned fish, slaw, and fresh toppings. Bright, bold, coastal.
