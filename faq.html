Frequently asked questions

FAQ

Quick answers before you head to the truck for fish and chips, tacos, lobster rolls, and whatever coastal specials are on deck today.

Where is Ad Mare Seafood located?

We are located at 1993 Robertson Rd. in Bells Corners, behind East India Company in Ottawa, Ontario.

What are your hours?

We operate seasonally from spring through fall. Our hours may vary throughout the week based on weather, events, and daily availability. Because our seafood is prepared daily, some menu items may sell out before closing.

For the most up-to-date schedule and availability, please check our latest Facebook or Instagram post before visiting.

Where can I park?

Free parking is available at our Bells Corners location at 1993 Robertson Road, behind East India Company.

Do menu items sell out?

Yes. Especially during busy weekends and peak summer periods. Because our seafood is prepared daily in limited quantities, some items may sell out before closing time.

We recommend visiting earlier in the day for the best selection.

What is Ad Mare known for?

Ad Mare is known for made-to-order seafood including crispy fish and chips, loaded fish tacos, lobster rolls (when they are in season), calamari, and other East Coast-inspired dishes served in Ottawa. All food is made to order on-site.

Where can I get lobster rolls in Ottawa?

Ad Mare serves lobster rolls seasonally in Ottawa, alongside other seafood favourites including fish tacos and calamari. Because lobster availability can vary throughout the year, lobster rolls are typically only offered during select periods of the season while supplies last.

Availability will always be announced on our social media pages and may be limited on busy days.

What comes with Ad Mare's Fish & Chips?

Ad Mare's classic fish and chips platters are served with truck-made coleslaw, a lemon wedge, tartar sauce, and our signature kettle chips (a medley of crispy potatos, sweet potatos, and beets).

If you prefer a more traditional option, you can request our French fries instead of truck chips. All sides are prepared fresh and made to complement our seafood dishes.

Does Ad Mare serve alcoholic beverages on the patio?

Yes. Alcoholic beverages (including liquor, cocktails, wine, and local beers) are available to order on the patio through our partners at East India Company, and will be brought directly to your table.

Do you serve dessert?

Yes. We serve key lime pie, a refreshing coastal classic that pairs perfectly with seafood. Dessert availability may vary throughout the season.

Do you cater private events?

Thank you for considering Ad Mare for your special event. Unfortunately, we are not available for catering at this time.

Do you offer reusable takeout containers?

Yes. Ad Mare is a Friendlier partner, so you can ask for reusable Friendlier packaging when you order.

Return your container through the Friendlier network (including the return box in front of the truck) so it can be washed, reused, and kept out of the single-use stream.
