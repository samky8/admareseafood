Frequently asked questions

FAQ

Quick answers before you head to the truck for fish and chips, tacos, lobster rolls, and whatever coastal favourite is on deck today.

Where is Ad Mare located?

Ad Mare Seafood is now located at 1993 Robertson Rd. in Bells Corners, behind East India Company in Ottawa, Ontario.

What are your hours?

Ad Mare operates seasonally from spring through fall. Our hours may vary throughout the week based on weather, events, and daily availability. Because our seafood is prepared fresh daily, some menu items may sell out before closing.

For the most up-to-date schedule and availability, please check our latest Facebook or Instagram post before visiting.

Where can I park?

Free parking is available at our Bells Corners location at 1993 Robertson Road, behind East India Company.

Do menu items sell out?

Yes. Especially during busy weekends and peak summer periods. Because our seafood is prepared daily in limited quantities, some items may sell out before closing time.

We recommend visiting earlier in the day for the best selection.

What is Ad Mare known for?

Ad Mare is known for made-to-order seafood including crispy fish and chips, loaded fish tacos, lobster rolls when they are in season, calamari, and other coastal-inspired dishes served in Ottawa.

Where can I get lobster rolls in Ottawa?

Ad Mare Seafood serves lobster rolls seasonally in Ottawa, alongside other seafood favourites including fish tacos and calamari.

Do you cater private events?

Unfortunately, Ad Mare is not available for catering at this time. Thank you for considering us.

Do you offer reusable takeout containers?

Yes. Ad Mare is a Friendlier partner, so you can ask for reusable Friendlier packaging when you order.

Return your container through the Friendlier network so it can be washed, reused, and kept out of the single-use stream.
